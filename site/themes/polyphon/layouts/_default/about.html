Polyphon

/ˈpɒl.i.fōn/  ·  POL-ee-fone

The name comes from polyphony — the musical practice of weaving multiple independent voices together into a single composition. In music, polyphony is what separates a lone melody from a full ensemble. Polyphon brings that same idea to AI: instead of a single model answering in isolation, multiple voices respond, react to each other, and build on what came before. Many voices. One conversation.
